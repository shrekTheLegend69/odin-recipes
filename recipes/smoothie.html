Smoothie

Description

Almost everyone likes a smoothie, but how to make a good one?

Ingredients

  • Fruit(s) of choice
  • Vegetable
  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Optional: Protein (nuts, peanut butter, protein powder etc.

Steps

  1. Chop your fruits and veggies.
  2. Blend them in a blender or smoothie mixer.
  3. Enjoy!
